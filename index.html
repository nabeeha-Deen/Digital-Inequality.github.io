TACKLING DIGITAL INEQUALITY




Technology
is a key resource of the Information Age [  ]


As a result, not having access to the internet and computing devices can make one feel isolated and unopportunate.

In 2020, amidst a global pandemic, a key adjustment to normality involved shifting from a physical world of work and eduction to a virtual one hosted on the internet. Though the switch had been relatively simple (albiet the initial struggle in famaliarising ourselves with new uses of existing technology) for us privileged that are able to surf the interweb at our fancy, it had not been the case for well over 759 million students around the world.




   
PURPOSE    

The purpose of this website is to educate people on the disparities tech inequality can cause, as no or limited access to the internet limits socio-economic progression. Solutions to solving this problem are large scale, but individuals can start to bring awareness to the issue through education.
